Scrolling-list fixtures

Start a region selection (Alt+Shift+R) and drag a rectangle that covers a scrolling box AND the empty page below it. Only the rows actually visible inside each box should be tinted and captured — rows scrolled (or clipped) out of view must be left out, even though their off-screen boxes sit under the rectangle.
